Learn About Computer Networks

Introduction

Ever wondered what makes your computer or phone connect to the world? Well, that's the magic of Computer Networks! A network is a system that links devices, allowing them to share data and resources. It acts as the backbone of the internet, apps, and communication. So, please stay on this website if you want to learn more!

Networking overview

A computer network is a group of interconnected devices that share resources and information. Networks enable communication, data transfer, and collaboration across the globe. From the internet to local office networks, they are the foundation of modern technology.

Wired Network

Wired networks use physical cables, like Ethernet, to connect devices. They offer high speed, reliability, and security compared to wireless networks. Common in offices and data centers, wired networks are ideal for stable, high-bandwidth connections.

Wireless Network

Wireless networks use radio waves, like Wi-Fi, to connect devices without cables. They provide flexibility and mobility, making them popular for homes, cafes, and mobile devices. However, they may face interference and security challenges.
